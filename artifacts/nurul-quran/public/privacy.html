Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Nurul Quran

Nurul Quran ("we", "our", "the app") is an Islamic education platform providing Quran recitation, lectures, courses, and Halal investment guidance. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you register, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed password.
  • Usage Data: Lectures played, courses enrolled in, favorites, and recently played items — linked to your account.
  • Halal Stock Preferences: Sector filters and watchlist items you select.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe, Razorpay processes your payment securely. We receive only a subscription confirmation — never card details.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type for analytics and optimization purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized Islamic education content
  • To remember your favorites, progress, and recently played content
  • To process premium subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To improve content quality and app performance
  • To send account-related notifications (if you have opted in)

3. Audio Content

Quran recitation audio is streamed from cdn.islamic.network — a public Islamic CDN. We do not host or store audio files ourselves. Your IP address may be logged by the CDN per their privacy policy.

4. Halal Stock Screener

Stock price data is fetched from Yahoo Finance for informational purposes only. Nurul Quran is not a SEBI-registered financial advisor. All stock information is for educational reference and should not be construed as investment advice. Please consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Razorpay: For payment processing (subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy)
  • cdn.islamic.network: For streaming Quran audio

6. Data Security

Passwords are stored as salted hashes using bcrypt. All data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases. We use HTTPS for all communications.

7. Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. You may also request data export or deletion by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Nurul Quran is suitable for all ages for educational content. However, features requiring account creation are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Offline Use

The app uses a Service Worker for offline functionality. Some content is cached locally on your device for performance. You can clear this cache through your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, please contact us through the app's support section or visit the Support page within the app.
