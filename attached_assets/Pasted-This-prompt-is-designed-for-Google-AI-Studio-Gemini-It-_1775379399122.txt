Prompt 2: The "Aesthetic Themes" System

"Create a 'Theme Manager' for my Next.js and Tailwind CSS app.

How to implement these "Smartly":
1. The "Viral" Share Tip
For the Aesthetic Customization to go viral, the shared image must look like a "Quote Card."

Layman Tip: Instead of just sharing text, use a library called html-to-image. It takes your beautiful themed card and turns it into a .jpg file automatically. When youth share that beautiful image on their Instagram Stories, they are essentially advertising your app for free!

2. The "Soul-Search" Database
You don't need a super-complex AI to start. You can create a simple Firestore Collection called soul_search with these fields:

problem_keyword: (e.g., "money", "lonely", "exams")

verse_ref: (e.g., "2:286")

modern_insight: (The 2-sentence boost)
