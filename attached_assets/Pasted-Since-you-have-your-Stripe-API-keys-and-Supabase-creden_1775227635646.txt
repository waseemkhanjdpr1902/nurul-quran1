Part 2: The Step-by-Step Deployment Process
Once the Replit Agent finishes the code, follow these exact steps to get it into the Google Play Store.

Step 1: Secure Your Secrets
In your Replit sidebar, click the Secrets (Padlock icon). Add these keys:

SUPABASE_URL

SUPABASE_ANON_KEY

STRIPE_SECRET_KEY

STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY

Step 2: The "Wrapper" (Converting Web to App)
Since the Play Store requires a .aab (Android App Bundle) file:

Go to PWABuilder.com.

Enter your Live Replit URL.

Click "Package for Store" -> Android.

Important: Under "Options," make sure you give your app a "Package ID" like com.yourname.nurulquran.

Download the Signed Digital Asset Link file and follow the instructions to place it in your Replit public/.well-known/ folder. This proves to Google that you own the site.

Step 3: Google Play Console Submission
Pay the $25 Fee: Sign up at the Google Play Console.

Upload the AAB: Upload the file you downloaded from PWABuilder.

App Content: You must fill out the "Data Safety" section. Tell Google:

"We collect Email addresses for account creation."

"Financial info is handled securely via Stripe."

Internal Testing: I recommend releasing to "Internal Testing" first. Add your own email, download it via the link Google gives you, and test the Stripe Donation button on your actual phone.

Step 4: Final Review
Submit for "Production." Google will review the app (usually 2–4 days). Once approved, NurulQuran will be live on the Play Store.
