Step 2: Critical "App-Ads.txt" Setup
Google will not pay you unless they can verify you own the app. This is the #1 reason ads stay "blank."

In your Replit project, create a new file in the public/ folder.

Name it exactly: app-ads.txt

Paste your specific AdMob line there (found in your AdMob Dashboard under Settings > Account Information).

Example: google.com, pub-1234567890123456, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0

Step 3: Google Play Console "Financial" Settings
When uploading a trading app with ads, Google requires specific disclosures.

Follow these settings in the Play Console:

App Content > Ads: Select "Yes, my app contains ads."

App Content > Financial Features: * Declare that your app provides "Stock/Trading Information."

Ensure you have a Disclaimer in your Store Description: "TradeMaster Pro is for educational purposes. We are not SEBI registered advisors." (This prevents Google from banning your ads for "unauthorized financial advice").

Data Safety: You must declare that you share "Device IDs" with "Third Parties" (this is for AdMob's personalized ads).
