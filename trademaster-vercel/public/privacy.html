Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

TradeMaster Pro

TradeMaster Pro ("we", "our", "the app") is a self-tracking SaaS tool designed for personal finance record-keeping. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Trade Journal Data: Trade entries you manually log (asset name, prices, quantity, notes). Stored by session ID in our database.
  • Session Identifier: A randomly generated session ID stored in your browser's localStorage. This is not linked to your identity.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to Elite, Razorpay processes your payment. We receive only your email and a subscription confirmation — we never store card numbers.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage metrics (page views, feature usage) to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the trading journal and analytics functionality
  • To process Elite subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To send subscription confirmation emails (if provided)
  • To improve app features and performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Razorpay: Payment processing (subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: We show ads to free users. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad personalization (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

4. Data Storage & Security

Trade journal data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases. We use industry-standard security practices. We do not store payment card details.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of all your journal data by contacting us. Since data is tied to an anonymous session ID, there is no account login required.

6. Investment Disclaimer

TradeMaster Pro is a self-tracking tool and is NOT a SEBI-registered investment advisor. All data in the app is for personal record-keeping and educational purposes only. We do not provide investment advice, trading recommendations, or financial guidance of any kind.

7. Children's Privacy

TradeMaster Pro is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, please contact us through the app's support section.
